# Dog Spa Harrow (Monika) - Business Information

## 1. Business Overview & Target Audience
* **Value Proposition:** "Does Your Dog Struggle with Grooming? You’re not alone — and it doesn’t have to be stressful. I offer gentle, one-to-one grooming for small and medium pets designed to support nervous, anxious, sensitive, elderly or disabled dogs. A calm, gentle approach to help your dog feel safe, relaxed and comfortable."
* **Core Differentiator:** 1-to-1 grooming basis. Limited appointments available each week to maintain a calm, stress-free environment.

## 2. Services

### Standard Grooming Services
* **Bath and Blow Dry:** Warm bath with coat-specific shampoo & conditioner, gentle ear and eye cleaning, blow dry & brush out. *(Note: Not suitable for dogs with matted coats, mats must be removed first).*
* **Mini Groom:** Perfect for a tidy-up between full grooms or as an introduction to grooming for pets over 6 months old. Includes warm bath, blow dry, clearing the eye area, feet/paw pads & hygiene trim, free nail trim, and aftercare advice.
* **Full Groom:** Tailored full grooming service designed around the dog’s individual needs. Includes warm bath, blow dry, de-shedding (where appropriate), paw pads & hygiene trim, full body styling, free nail trim, and aftercare advice.

### Puppy Grooming Introduction (3–6 months)
A 3-session programme to build confidence from the first visit at the puppy's pace.
* **Session 1 (approx. 30 mins):** Calm introduction to the salon, gentle handling, and getting used to the environment.
* **Session 2 (approx. 45–60 mins):** Bath, gentle dry & light brushing, introduction to grooming tools and sounds.
* **Session 3 (approx. 60–75 mins):** Bath, dry & brush, face/feet & hygiene tidy (as tolerated), nail trim.

### Spa Add-ons & Treatments
* **Facial Massage:** Calming facial treatment using tear-free products.
* **Nose and Paw Balm Massage:** Nourishes dry areas with pet-safe balm.
* **Deep Fur De-Shedding Spa Treatment:** Uses Madra Mor Shed Safely Dog Spa Mud to release undercoat and detox skin.
* **Deep Skin Soothing and Nourishing Treatment:** Uses Madra Mor Soothing Dog Spa Mud to soothe irritated skin and reduce hot spots.
* **Nail Trim and File:** Free with Mini/Full Grooms; available as an add-on.
* **Teeth Brushing:** Gentle service to reduce plaque and freshen breath.
